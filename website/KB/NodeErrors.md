## Node Related Errors

These messages usually related to issues with individual nodes.

### Specific MessageID Results

#### MessageID 42

A message was sent to the node and it was not acknowledged, and the device does not support the WakeUP CC, so its not a traditional sleeping device (Not to be confused with a FLiRS device). This could indicate communication issues, the Node offline, or other problems. This message is usually accompanied by other messages that will assist pinpointing the problems. Please consult [DeadNode](deadnode) for further information.
